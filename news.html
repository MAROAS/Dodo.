ACTIVITIES

At the end of every two years we have general meeting with all members from different conners.
The general forum will be held in a place where people can have access on different facilities, for example, quick access on transport if a member does not have his own transport, all our SELSO members will be advised to lodge in a near place for not being late in the meeting.


Most of the Topics which will be discussed in the meeting are the issues impacted on the business during the period of two years:


The mode of production,
Customers' behaviour
Market place challenges
New system for a maximum income
Expansion of the company
Demographic impact
Means of Distribution. etc...


All the information must be well prepared and transmitted in an understandable way.
Members have the right to ask any questions and must be responded in a sufficient way by the person in charge to make sure there is clarity of Business.
